
[[Confidentiality]]
	Ensures that information is only accessible to those with appropriate authorization.
		-Encryption.



[[Integrity]]
	Ensures data remains accurate and unaltered unless modification is required.
		Checksums.



[[Availability]]
	Ensures that information and resources are accessible and functional when needed by authorized users.
		Redundancy measures.


[[Non-repudiation]]
Guaranteeing that a specific action or even has taken place and cannot be denied by the parties involved.
	If I send an email and I signed, deniability will not be possible because the digital signature is there.


[[Authentication]]
	Process of verifying the identity of a user or system
