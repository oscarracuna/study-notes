
**SWITCHES**
They act as a controller. They connect computers, printers and servers to a **network in a building or campus.**
- **Managed on-premises**: Lets you configure and monitor your LAN.
- **Cloud managed:** Simplifies management for small IT teams. UI, multisite full-stack management and automatic updates.

**ROUTERS**
They connect **multiple networks together** and they connect computers to those networks to the internet. They enable all networked computers to **share** a single **internet** **connection.** They provide **bandwidth.**
![[Pasted image 20240124111533.png]]

**Access points**
It allows devices to connect to the **Wireless network** without cables. It also acts as an amplifier for the network so many devices are able to connect to the network from farther away.
