Ensures data remains accurate and unaltered unless modification is required.
		Checksums.

Helps to ensure information and data remain accurate and unchanged from their original state unless intentionally modified by an authorized individual.

- Verifies the accuracy and trustworthiness of data over the entire lifecycle.

We want to:
- [[Ensure data accuracy]].
- [[Maintain trust]].
- [[Ensure system operability]].

To maintain the integrity of our data we are going to use 5 methods:
- [[Hashing]]
- [[Digital signatures]]
- [[Checksums]]
- [[Access Controls]]
- [[Regular Audits]]
